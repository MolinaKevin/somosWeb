How does SOMOS work?

A simple system that benefits consumers, businesses and organizations alike.

Consumers

  • Earn points by shopping at participating businesses
  • Redeem points for discounts and benefits
  • See in real time how your purchases generate donations

Businesses

  • Donate a small percentage of your sales
  • Gain digital visibility on the platform
  • Build customer loyalty with the points system

Organizations

  • Receive constant donations
  • Increase your visibility in the community
  • Act as communication channels

Frequently asked questions

We answer your questions about SOMOS

Simply make purchases at any of SOMOS's participating businesses. When paying, show your user code in the app and you will automatically accumulate points proportional to the value of your purchase.

The percentage varies by business, but generally ranges between 1% and 5% of the value of each sale. This percentage is decided by each business when registering on the platform.

Organizations are carefully selected by our team, verifying their transparency, social impact and real needs. We also consider suggestions from the community and from the participating businesses themselves.

Absolutely not! For consumers, SOMOS is completely free. Points are generated with your regular purchases at no additional cost to you.

In the SOMOS app you'll find a catalog of benefits and discounts that you can redeem with your accumulated points. Simply select the benefit you want and show the generated code to the corresponding business.
